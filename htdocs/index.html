Lecture Listings

Information about lectures provided by Faculties for the academic year 2012-13 is available here, either in the form of direct links to Faculty or departmental websites, or for subjects participating in the pilot system, a calendar version which can be viewed as a report, a weekly timetable or added to your own calendar by clicking the relevant link on a timetable's page.

Information is accessed via the drop-down boxes, and is presented under the Faculty responsible for the course, with the exception of the Natural Sciences and Medical and Veterinary Sciences Triposes which have their own listings. An additional drop-down box lists all courses in alphabetical order with MPhils grouped together.

Natural Sciences Tripos Part IA and Part IB

Please note that timetables include all possible practicals; for Part IA you will be allocated to particular slots according to your choice of subjects, for Part IB you will arrange specific times during introductory sessions on 2nd & 3rd October.

Extra Information

The University offers a large number of lectures, seminars, and other events, many of which are free of charge, to members of the University and others who are interested. Details can be found on Faculty and Departmental websites, and in the following resources:

What's On carries details of exhibitions, music, theatre and film, courses, and workshops, and is searchable by category and date. RSS and iCal feeds, and a subscription email service are available.

Talks.cam is a fully searchable talks listing service, and talks can be subscribed to and details downloaded.

University of Cambridge Training is a central point for staff and students of the University and its Colleges to search for and book onto a variety of training courses run by participating University training providers

For help, contact timetables-support@caret.cam.ac.uk
